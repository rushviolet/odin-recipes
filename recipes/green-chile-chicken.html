Green Chile Chicken

Description

This is a pressure cooker recipe using a variety of fresh green peppers, tomatillos, spices, and chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken breast
  • 4 tomatillos
  • 2 poblano peppers
  • 2 anaheim peppers
  • ground cumin to taste
  • lost interest in listing ingredients
  • but the real recipe is very good whether you sub chicken breasts or not

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in pressure cooker.
  2. Cook at high pressure for 15 minutes.
  3. Natural release, serve over rice and garnish.
